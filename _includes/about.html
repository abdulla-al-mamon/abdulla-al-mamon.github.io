About

He is currently an Assistant Professor at the Department of Physics, in Vivekananda Satavarshiki Mahavidyalaya (VSM) [affiliated to the Vidyasagar University], Jhargram, India. Before joining VSM as a permanent faculty member, he was a National Post-Doctoral Fellow at Jadavpur University, Kolkata, India. 

He received his Ph.D degree from the Visva-Bharati (A Central University and an Institution of National Importance),   India on 10th November, 2016. The title of his PhD thesis is, ``Aspects of Scalar Field Models of Dark Energy''. He is a Life Member of Indian Association for General Relativity and Gravitation, since 2013.
